VEG RAMEN

Ramen is a noodle soup that originated in Japan, but its exact origins are unclear. It's thought to have been introduced to Japan between the 17th and 20th centuries, and may have its roots in Chinese-style noodles.

INGREDIENTS

  • Ramen noodles (1 pack, instant or fresh)
  • Vegetable broth (2 cups)
  • sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Mixed vegetables (like carrots, bell peppers, spinach, or mushrooms - 1 cup
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)

PROCESS

  • Cook the noodles: Boil the ramen noodles according to the package instructions and set them aside.
  • Prepare the broth: In a pot, heat the vegetable broth, soy sauce, and minced garlic. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  • Add veggies: Add your choice of mixed vegetables to the broth and cook until tender.
  • Assemble: Add the cooked noodles to the broth and let it soak up the flavors for 1-2 minutes.
  • Serve: Pour into bowls and enjoy your simple and delicious veg ramen!
